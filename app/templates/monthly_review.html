Preparation

The Routine

Follow these steps to ensure your monthly numbers are accurate before locking them in.

1
Check your accounts

Log in to your banking apps and check if your expected contributions (pension, ISA, etc.) have actually left your account.

2
Confirm or Skip

Use the "Confirmed contributions" section below to tick off what happened. If you didn't contribute this month, hit Skip.

3
Update manual balances

For accounts Shelly can't track automatically (like some pensions or savings), update their balance in the "Manual updates" section.

4
Mark as Complete

Once everything is updated, hit "Mark as Complete" at the top to lock in this month's snapshot.
